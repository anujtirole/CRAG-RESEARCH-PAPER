Aurelio De Laurentiis

Aurelio De Laurentiis (born 24 May 1949 in Rome) is a prominent Italian film producer through his company, Filmauro and the current chairman of Italian football club Napoli and Bari. In 1995, he was a member of the jury at the 19th Moscow International Film Festival. He is the son of film producer Luigi De Laurentiis, nephew of film producer Dino De Laurentiis and first-cousin once removed of chef Giada De Laurentiis.
