Jay Jennings

Jay Robert Jennings( born August 23, 1965) is an American independent filmmaker and author. He has directed two feature films," Loanshark"( 1999) and" Hell to Pay"( 2014), as well as, an assortment of short films and documentaries. Jennings uses handheld cameras and cinéma vérité techniques, shooting his films among old Hollywood buildings and streets.
