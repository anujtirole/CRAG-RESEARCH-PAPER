Raffaella De Laurentiis

Raffaella De Laurentiis( born 28 June 1954) is an Italian film producer. Films that she has produced include" Conan the BarbarianConan the DestroyerDunePrancer", all films in the" Dragonheart" series," The Forbidden Kingdom" and" Kull the Conqueror". She is the daughter of film producer Dino De Laurentiis and actress Silvana Mangano. She is the sister of Veronica De Laurentiis, who is the mother of Food Network chef Giada De Laurentiis. She appeared in eighteen episodes of Giada's show" Giada at Home".
