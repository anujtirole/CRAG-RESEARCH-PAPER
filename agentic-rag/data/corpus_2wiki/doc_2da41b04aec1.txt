Scot Armstrong

Scot Armstrong is an American screenwriter, director, and producer. He is credited with writing or co-writing numerous comedy films, including" Old SchoolSemi- ProRoad Trip", and many others. He is also the writer and director of the 2015 film," Search Party". The film will be released in the US in May 2016. Also in 2016, his TV series," Dice", premiered on Showtime.
