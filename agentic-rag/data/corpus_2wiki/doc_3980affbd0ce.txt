John Gordon, 3rd Earl of Aboyne

John Gordon, 3rd Earl of Aboyne( born April 1700, died 7 April 1732) was the son of Charles Gordon, 2nd Earl of Aboyne and Elizabeth Lyon. He succeeded his father as 3rd Earl of Aboyne in April 1702. On his death in 1732 he was succeeded in his titles by his eldest son.
