Alexandra, Princess of Leiningen

Princess Alexandra of Hanover, Duchess of Brunswick- Lüneburg, Princess of Leiningen( born 18 February 1959), is the wife of Andreas, 8th Prince of Leiningen. Born in Hanover, Lower Saxony, Germany, Alexandra is the youngest daughter of Ernest Augustus, Prince of Hanover( 1914 – 1987), and his wife, Princess Ortrud of Schleswig- Holstein- Sonderburg- Glücksburg( 1925–1980). Her eldest brother is Ernst August, Prince of Hanover, current head of the House of Hanover and her sister- in- law is Princess Caroline of Monaco.
