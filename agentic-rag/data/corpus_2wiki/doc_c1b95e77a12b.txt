John FitzGerald, 4th Earl of Desmond

John FitzGerald, 4th Earl of Desmond( died 1399) was the son of Gerald FitzGerald, 3rd Earl of Desmond. He married and had one son, Thomas, who succeeded him as Earl of Desmond. According to Burke, John FitzGerald married Joan Roche, the daughter of Lord Fermoy. In March 1399 FitzGerald drowned at Ardfinnan on the River Suir, and was buried at Youghal. He was succeeded by his son Thomas FitzJohn FitzGerald, 5th Earl of Desmond.
