Pepin III, Count of Vermandois

Pepin III, Count of Vermandois( also known as Pepin II, Count of Senlis; French:" Pépin")( c. 846 – 893) was a Frankish noble; the Count of Senlis and Count of Vermandois( 850 – 893); Lord of Valois( 877 – 886), and later Count of Valois( 886 – 893). He was a son of Pepin, Count of Vermandois and Valois and thus a grandson of Bernard of Italy, who was himself a grandson of Charlemagne.
