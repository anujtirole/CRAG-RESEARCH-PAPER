Themmadi Velappan

Themmadi Velappan is a 1976 Indian Malayalam film, directed by Hariharan and produced by G. P. Balan. It's remake of Kannada superhit movie Sampathige Saval. The film stars Prem Nazir, Madhu, Jayabharathi and KPAC Lalitha in the lead roles. It was a super hit. The film has a musical score by M. S. Viswanathan. The movie is a remake of 1974 Kannada movie" Sampathige Savaal" starring Rajkumar.
