Frederick O'Brien

Frederick O'Brien( 1869- 1932) was an American author, journalist, hobo, peripatetic world traveler, and public administrator. He wrote three best- selling travel books about French Polynesia between 1919 and 1922:" White Shadows in the South Seas, Mystic Isles of the South Seas," and" Atolls of the Sun." A movie was made in 1928 of" White Shadows in the South Seas".
