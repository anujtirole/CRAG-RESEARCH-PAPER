Crow Foot

Crow Foot (1873 – December 15, 1890) was the son of Sitting Bull of the Lakota. His mother was either Seen by Her Nation or Four Robes. He had sisters named Standing Holy and Lodge; he also had brothers named Henry, Little Soldier, Red Scout, and Theodore. In 1881 he participated alongside his father in the surrender at Fort Buford. Crow Foot was killed along with his father on December 15, 1890, by a group of Indian agents.
