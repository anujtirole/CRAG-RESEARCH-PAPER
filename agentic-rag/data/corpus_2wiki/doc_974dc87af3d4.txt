Christian Albrecht, Prince of Hohenlohe-Langenburg

Christian Albrecht, 2nd Prince of Hohenlohe- Langenburg( 27 March 1726 in Langenburg – 4 July 1789 in Ludwigsruhe), was the second ruling Prince of Hohenlohe- Langenburg and a Dutch lieutenant- general. He was the first child of Ludwig, Prince of Hohenlohe- Langenburg and Countess Eleonore of Nassau- Saarbrücken. On 7 January 1764, his father was elevated to an Imperial Prince by Emperor Francis I.
