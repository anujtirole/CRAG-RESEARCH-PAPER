The Curse of Vererbung

The Curse of Vererbung( German: Der Fluch der Vererbung)(" The Curse of Heredity: Those Who Should Not Be Mothers") is a 1927 German silent film directed by Adolf Trotz and starring Marcella Albani, Maly Delschaft and Carl de Vogt. The film's sets were designed by the art director Leopold Blonder.
