The New Adventures of the Elusive Avengers

The New Adventures of the Elusive Avengers( translit. " Novye prikluchenya Neulovimykh") is a 1968 Soviet action movie, a sequel of" The Elusive Avengers", directed by Edmond Keosayan and made on Mosfilm. The movie was followed by" The Crown of the Russian Empire, or Once Again the Elusive Avengers" released in 1971.
