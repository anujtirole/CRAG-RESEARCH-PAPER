Martha De Laurentiis

Martha De Laurentiis( born 10 July 1954 as" Martha Schumacher") is an American film producer. She was credited as Martha Schumacher until 1995. She is known for producing films such as" Breakdown Hannibal" and" Red Dragon" with her late husband Dino De Laurentiis, and TV series" Hannibal". In 1980, she and Dino De Laurentiis founded the production company the Dino De Laurentiis Company, and she is currently the company's chairman. With her husband Dino, whom she married in 1990, she had two daughters, Carolyna and Dina. De Laurentiis was a jury member at the 65th Berlin International Film Festival. On December 16, 2017, she was awarded an honorary doctorate of humanities by her alma mater, Ball State University.
